Agile development

Consisting of several iterative and incremental software development methodologies, agile development allows us to be flexible. Building software can and should be flexible. Just in time information, quick changes and changing requirements.

Early experimentation

The envisioned product should be put in front of the end-users as early as possible. Doing so in the first stages of developing the product will exposes valuable information. May it be usability testing, A/B testing or just simply a brief talk if it shapes the vision of the product for both the user and the developing team, it's adding value.

Feedback loops

Every click on a button could provide important information for the next step in product development. A constant flow of information is vital to effective software development. By combining both quantitative and qualitative research methods.
